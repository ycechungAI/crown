Performs a raycast against objects in the scene.

The ray is casted from position from, has direction dir and is long length If any actor is hit along the ray, EventStream is filled according to mode previously specified and callback will be called for processing. CollisionMode::ANY: the callback is called with just true or false depending on whether the ray hit anything or not. CollisionMode::CLOSEST: the first argument will tell if there was a hit or not, as before. If there was a hit, the callback will also be called with the position of the hit, the distance from the origin, the normal of the surface that was hit and the actor that was hit. CollisionMode::ALL: as CollisionMode::CLOSEST, with more tuples
